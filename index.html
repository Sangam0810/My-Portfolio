About Me

Who I am

I'm a passionate Full Stack Developer and Electronics & Communication Engineer with hands-on experience in designing and developing full-fledged web applications and IoT systems. My technical proficiency lies in the MERN stack — MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js — allowing me to craft responsive, scalable, and high-performance applications that solve real-world problems.

On the frontend, I build sleek and user-friendly interfaces using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React.js, and Redux. I’m well-versed in responsive design techniques, ensuring my applications work seamlessly across all devices using Flexbox, CSS Grid, and media queries.

On the backend, I specialize in building RESTful APIs using Node.js and Express.js, coupled with MongoDB for efficient data storage. I also implement secure authentication systems using JWT and bcrypt, and manage full-stack deployments on platforms like Render and Netlify.

In addition to web development, I have academic and project-based experience in IoT and embedded systems, blending my electronics background with software skills. My projects demonstrate my ability to integrate sensors, microcontrollers, and cloud platforms to create smart and automated systems.

I am known for my attention to detail, strong problem-solving mindset, and dedication to continuous learning. Whether working independently or in a team, I bring energy, innovation, and a clear focus on delivering impactful digital solutions.
